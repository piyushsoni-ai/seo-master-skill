## What is SEO Master?

SEO Master is a portable, file-based AI skill that turns any compatible AI agent into a
senior SEO strategist. It covers the full SEO lifecycle — from technical audits to
client-ready reports — without fluff, fake guarantees, or generic checklists.

---

## What it can do

- ✅ Full technical SEO audits
- ✅ On-page, content & E-E-A-T audits
- ✅ Keyword research, clustering & intent mapping
- ✅ Competitor gap & SERP analysis
- ✅ Local SEO & Google Business Profile optimization
- ✅ Backlink auditing, scoring & outreach
- ✅ AEO / GEO — optimize for AI answer engines
- ✅ Entity SEO & topical authority building
- ✅ 30 / 60 / 90-day growth plans
- ✅ Client-ready reports & proposals
- ✅ Industry playbooks (dental, medical, ecommerce, SaaS, real estate, local)
- ✅ Apify-first with native scraping fallback

## How to use

### In Claude Projects
1. Create a new Claude Project
2. Upload the entire `seo-master` folder as project knowledge
3. Start with: `Audit this website: [URL]`

### In Codex / Cursor / OpenClaw
Add the skill folder to your agent's context or knowledge path and reference `SKILL.md` as the primary instruction file.
